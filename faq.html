Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply add your favorite products to the cart and click "Order on WhatsApp." We'll confirm your order directly with you.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are usually delivered within 3–5 business days across Pakistan.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD).

Can I exchange my order?

Yes. Exchanges are available only for damaged or incorrect items. Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order.

How do I care for my satin accessories?

Hand wash gently with cold water, avoid bleach, and air dry to maintain the quality of your accessories.
