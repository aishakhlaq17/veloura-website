Privacy Policy

At Veloura, your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring it is handled responsibly.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to process your order, including:

We take reasonable measures to keep your personal information secure and confidential. By placing an order with Veloura, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
