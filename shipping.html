Shipping Policy

At Veloura, we carefully package every order to ensure your accessories arrive safely and in perfect condition.

Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after order confirmation.
  • Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

Delivery Time

  • Major Cities: 2–4 business days
  • Other Areas: 3–7 business days

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to public holidays, weather conditions, or courier delays.

Shipping Charges

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): PKR 300
  • Shipping charges may vary for remote or hard-to-reach locations.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, we will share your tracking details via WhatsApp so you can easily track your package.

If you have any questions regarding your order or shipping, feel free to contact us through WhatsApp. We're always happy to help.
